Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? ‚ñº

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused products in their original packaging. For damaged or defective items, we provide free returns and exchanges. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take? ‚ñº

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. All orders are shipped carbon-neutral through our partnership with climate offset programs.

Are your products truly eco-friendly? ‚ñº

Yes! Every product in our store is carefully vetted for sustainability. We evaluate materials, manufacturing processes, packaging, and end-of-life disposal. Look for our certification badges on product pages for specific environmental credentials.

Do you ship internationally? ‚ñº

Currently, we ship to the United States, Canada, UK, and EU countries. International shipping typically takes 10-14 business days. We're working to expand our shipping regions while maintaining our carbon-neutral commitment.

How can I partner with EcoMart? ‚ñº

We're always looking for sustainable brands and creators to partner with. Please fill out the contact form above with "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject, and include details about your brand and products. Our partnerships team will review your submission within 5-7 business days.
