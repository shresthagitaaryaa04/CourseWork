Design Research

Comparative Analysis

Exploring sustainable e-commerce design through competitive research and user-centered thinking

Research Methodology

To create an effective sustainable e-commerce platform, we conducted comprehensive competitive analysis of leading eco-friendly online stores. This research examines design patterns, user experience strategies, and conversion optimization techniques specifically within the sustainable products market.

Our analysis focuses on understanding what makes sustainable e-commerce sites both commercially successful and aligned with environmental values, identifying gaps in current market offerings, and discovering opportunities for innovation.

Our Design Decisions

Visual Identity

We chose a natural, organic aesthetic using earthy greens and neutral tones. Unlike the bright, playful approach of sites like The Good Fill or the premium minimalism of Package Free, our design feels grounded and approachable‚Äîreflecting the natural materials we promote.

Rationale: Our target audience values authenticity over polish. A natural aesthetic signals genuine commitment to environment rather than greenwashing.

Typography

We paired the serif font Lora for headings with the clean sans-serif Work Sans for body text. This combination provides warmth and readability while avoiding the generic feel of system fonts used by many competitors.

Rationale: Serif headings add timeless elegance suggesting quality and craftsmanship, while sans-serif body maintains modern web usability.

Planned Improvements

Based on competitive research and user feedback, we're developing these enhancements:

Partner with local shops for product refill stations, combining The Good Fill's model with our marketplace approach.

Research Conclusion

Competitive analysis reveals that sustainable e-commerce success requires balancing multiple factors: beautiful design that reflects environmental values, transparent communication about sourcing and impact, educational content that builds trust, and practical features that make sustainable choices convenient.

ECO differentiates through local focus, artisan partnerships, affordability mission, and radical transparency. While we learn from global leaders like Patagonia, Package Free, and EarthHero, our South Asian context demands unique solutions. We're not simply replicating Western sustainable e-commerce‚Äîwe're pioneering an approach that honors local traditions, supports regional artisans, and makes sustainability accessible to our community.
